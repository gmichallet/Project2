Correlation Between Age and COVID Vaccine Adverse Effects


COVID Vaccine Visualization Dashboard


The purpose of this dashboard is to help visualize the adverse effects associated with COVIID-19 vaccinations

The data used for this dashboard is primarly made up of adults, the majority of which are
between 25 and 95 years of age. More females have recieved vaccines versus males but more males
have died after recieving the vaccine. We also have provided general Covid-19 data to show the
overall fatality ratio by state and positive Covid-19 cases over the past year for reference.

This dashboard provides the source data and visualizations, as well as analysis, explanations, and observed correlations.


The visualizations are available in the dropdown menu bar
or by clicking either the charts button or visualizations slides bellow.
