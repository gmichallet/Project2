Here are some examples of 2 states that have been heavily impacted by Covid-19

Illinois and New York were two states that were hit hard by Covid-19. Both states had 2 diffinitive waves, the second of which was worse for both. In both states the second waves have started to tapper off. Whether or not this is due to the wide rolllout of vaccinations we can't be sure, but we do know that vaccinations became available at the start of 2021 and that seems to be the start of the decrease in positive Covid-19 in both states.
